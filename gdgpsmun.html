G.D. Goenka Public School MUN

About the event

G.D. Goenka Public School, as a part of its aim of providing a holistic education, is proud to announce the arrival of its maiden edition of G.D. Goenka Public School Model United Nations.

GDGPSMUN is to be held on 25th and 26th of November 2017.

What I had done

In G.D. Goenka Public School Model United Nations I was the Under seceratory general for Information Technology. I had to design the logo and social media stuff. I also had to manage registrations and was assisted by Organizing Committee members working under me for same.
