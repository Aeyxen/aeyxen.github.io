NewsSense

Revoulutionarizing the way you get news

What is it?

NewsSense is a news app concept that aims at giving news by analyzing your interest and mood. This idea if executed will help in making earth a better place, continue reading if you wanna know what problem lead to development of this concept.

The Problem

Today's news apps analyze your interests and only give you the news from point of view that you love to listen, its certainly a sad thing as this makes people fall in traps of extremism. This makes people more obessed with their own ideology and they stop respecting ideas of others. An example is shown through the following TED video where Mr. Eli Pariser tells that internet is now no longer neutral and only shows you what you want. This kinda stuff is certainly not god for a democracy.

The Solution

The solution to fight the problem is to redefine the approach of using the technologies, I not being a Neo-Luddist am surely not saying to stop usage of your search history to analyze your interests. But have another approah to see this issue, seeing rise of IoT
